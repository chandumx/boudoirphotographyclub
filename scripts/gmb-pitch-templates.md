## PERSONALIZATION TIPS
Before sending, quickly check their GMB profile:
1. Google "[Studio Name] [City]" — do they appear in the map pack?
2. Click their profile — count the photos (under 20 = flag it)
3. Check for recent posts (none in 60+ days = flag it)
4. Check review count (under 25 = flag it)
5. Check if they have a website linked (missing = flag it)

Mention 1–2 specific issues in the email. This proves you actually looked
and makes the email feel personal, not mass-sent.

## OUTREACH TOOL RECOMMENDATIONS
DO NOT use personal Gmail for bulk sends — risk of suspension.

Use one of these instead (all have free tiers):
- Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) — free up to 300 emails/day
- Mailchimp — free up to 500 contacts
- Instantly.ai — best for cold email, built-in warmup
- Apollo.io — has email finder + sequencing

Send max 30–50 emails/day to stay out of spam filters.
Always include your name, website, and a clear way to opt out.
